## takeaways

- alternate push and pull days
- push days:
	- chest press 
		- variation: pec crossover (https://youtu.be/bYnZK5kN2uo?si=5GQ9LCy5UG1Ryxw0)
	- tricep press
	- overhead press
	- front squat
		- variation: split squats (single leg, like a lunge)
- pull days:
	- dead lift
	- front row
	- bicep curls
	- calf raise
